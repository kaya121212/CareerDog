## What Is This?

CareerDog is a Chrome extension for job hunters who are tired of filling out the same form over and over like some kind of digital Groundhog Day. It reads job listings so you don't have to, fills in your info so your fingers don't have to, and clips job details to your clipboard so your brain doesn't have to.

You're welcome.

---

## Features

- **Copy Job** — Grabs the job title, company name, and full description. Perfect for pasting into ChatGPT and saying "write me a cover letter that sounds like I'm passionate about B2B SaaS."
- **Save Job** — Copies company, title, and URL as a neat tab-separated line, ready to paste into Google Sheets like the organized professional you pretend to be.
- **Autofill** — Fills in your name, email, phone, LinkedIn, and address on application forms. Reclaim those 45 seconds per application. Multiply by 200 applications. That's like two free vacation days.

## Installation

1. Download or clone this repo
2. Go to `chrome://extensions` in Chrome
3. Flip on **Developer mode** (top-right corner)
4. Hit **Load unpacked** → select this folder
5. Pin the 🐶 to your toolbar so it's ready when you need it at 11pm on a Sunday

---

## How To Use It

**Copy Job** — Open any job listing, click the icon, hit Copy Job. Job title, company, and description land on your clipboard. Go nuts.

**Save Job** — Same deal, but outputs `Company [tab] Title [tab] URL` for pasting into a spreadsheet. Because tracking rejections in a spreadsheet builds character.

**Autofill** — Open an application form, click the icon, hit Autofill. Watch your info appear like magic. Feel briefly powerful.

**Profile** — Click Profile to store your details. Saved locally in your browser. Nobody's stealing your zip code.
